Our Process

We do the hard work.
You do the parenting.

Finding exceptional childcare is a full-time job in itself. Our concierge service removes every step of the process — from research to references to placement.

01

Tell us about your family

A 20-minute discovery call where we learn your schedule, values, preferences, and non-negotiables. No forms. No tick boxes.

02

We search the market for you

Our team scours our verified network and finds candidates that genuinely fit — not just whoever's available.

03

We present your shortlist

You receive a curated shortlist of 3–5 vetted, referenced, DBS-checked options with our full assessment.

04

We place and support

We handle contracts, trial days, and onboarding. And we're on hand for the first 90 days to make sure everything's perfect.

Typical Timeline
Day 1
Discovery call completed. Family brief sent to our network.
Day 3–5
Candidates interviewed & referenced by our team.
Day 7
Shortlist presented with detailed profiles & our recommendation.
Day 10–14
Trial day arranged. Contract drafted. Start date confirmed.
Day 14+
Your family is in safe, trusted hands. We check in at 30, 60 & 90 days.

who lived the problem.

The Childcare Broker was founded because finding great childcare was broken. Parents were left to wade through unvetted platforms, generic listings, and contradictory advice — all while trying to run a home, a career, and a life.

We built a service that treats childcare the way it deserves to be treated: as one of the most important decisions a family will ever make. With proper vetting, personal guidance, and genuine accountability.

Every family we work with gets the same level of care we'd want for our own children. That's not a marketing line. It's the only way we know how to work.
